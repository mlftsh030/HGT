Overview

See the “Big 3” of Cape Town in a single day: Table Mountain, Cape Point, and the African penguins at Boulders Beach. This full-day tour takes you along the scenic Peninsula coastline, combining the region's most iconic natural landmarks into one comfortable, guided day out.

With door-to-door pickup and an air-conditioned vehicle between stops, this is the easiest way to experience the full Cape Peninsula without organising transport and tickets yourself. See Rules & Requirements below before you book.

Rules & Requirements

The Table Mountain cableway closes in high wind or low visibility. If it's closed, we'll adjust the itinerary and offer an alternative where possible.
This is a full 8–9 hour day — please let us know about any time constraints when booking.
Some walking is involved at each stop, particularly at Cape Point and Boulders Beach — comfortable shoes are recommended.
Tours run in small groups for a more personal experience and easier logistics at each stop.

What to Bring

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • A warm layer — it can be windy at Cape Point and on the cableway
  • Sunblock, hat and sunglasses
  • Camera
Not Included
  • Meals and drinks
  • Gratuities for your guide

Tour Plan

We collect you from the V&A Waterfront or your hotel in the CBD for an early start.
Ride the cableway to the top of Table Mountain for panoramic views over the city and coastline.
Drive the scenic Peninsula coastline to Cape Point, with time to explore the lighthouse and lookout points.
Visit the African penguin colony at Boulders Beach before the drive back to Cape Town.

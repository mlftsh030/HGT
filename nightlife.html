Before You Book — House Rules

  • A physical South African ID or original passport is mandatory for entry at every venue — no exceptions, no digital copies.
  • The legal minimum age is 18, but several premium venues enforce a 21+ or 23+ door policy — check each venue's page.
  • Smart-casual dress is expected at upscale lounges; sportswear and flip-flops can mean denied entry.
  • Right of admission is reserved by every venue's own security — our role is transport, bookings and a guide for the night, not the door policy.

Overview

Saddle up and explore the shifting white sand dunes of the Witzands Aquifer Nature Reserve at Atlantis, with Table Mountain visible on the horizon and just 45 minutes from Cape Town. Our quad biking experience takes you along designated trails across dunes that rise up to 50 metres high, with an experienced guide leading the way. No experience is necessary — every group starts with a full safety briefing and a practice run before heading out onto the dunes.

Because Witzands is a protected nature reserve, all riding stays on marked trails to protect the rare fynbos of the Cape Floristic Region, and every guest needs a conservation permit to enter — see Rules & Requirements below before you book.

Rules & Requirements

Every guest must carry an original South African ID or a physical passport to enter the reserve — digital copies are not accepted, and without one you will be turned away at the gate with no refund. A Witzands conservation permit (R250 per person) is also compulsory and is paid separately from your tour fee.
Riders must be at least 16 years old to drive on our tours. Children aged 4–12 may join as a ride-along on a fit-for-purpose two-seater bike with a guide or parent; children under 4 cannot enter the reserve. Riders are limited to a maximum weight of 140kg.
No alcohol is permitted anywhere in the reserve, before or during your ride. Bring water or a non-alcoholic drink to stay hydrated. Riders under the influence of alcohol or drugs will not be allowed to ride.
Witzands protects rare fynbos and wildlife found only in the Cape Floristic Region, so all riding stays strictly on designated trails. Littering or disturbing plants and wildlife can result in immediate expulsion from the reserve without a refund.

What to Bring

  • Original South African ID or physical passport
  • Closed-toe shoes
  • Water or a non-alcoholic drink
  • Sunblock and sunglasses
  • Cash or card for the R250pp conservation permit at the gate

Tour Plan

We meet at Atlantis Dunes where your guide runs through a full safety briefing and fits everyone with a helmet and gear before getting started.
Get kitted out with a helmet and gear, then take your quad bike for a short practice run on flat ground before heading onto the dunes.
Follow your guide along the designated trails across the sweeping white sand dunes, for the riding time you booked — 30, 45, or 60 minutes — with regular stops to take in the views and catch your breath.
Head back to base to return your gear, with time to relax and chat with your guide before you head off.

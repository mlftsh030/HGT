Overview

The sophisticated, high-energy side of Cape Town nightlife — futuristic sound systems, upscale cocktail menus, and guestlist entry to the clubs and lounges that define the City Bowl and East City after dark. This package bundles secure city transport with entry across a guestlist route, so you move between venues without queues or door hassle.

The Urban Groove Package

R1,200 / person, all-in

A guestlist night across the city's top CBD venues.

  • Secure transport between venues, City Bowl & East City
  • Guestlist entry to three venues on your chosen route
  • Two signature cocktails included
  • A guide with you for the night to handle logistics & bookings

Venues on This Package

Choose three stops for your guestlist route when you book.

HALO Nightclub
3.9 · City Centre 71 Loop St, City Centre

A futuristic two-storey club — the Groove Lounge for cocktails upstairs, and a world-class Funktion-One sound system with 3D lightscapes in the Main Room below.

Modular Club
4.3 · City Centre Riebeek St, City Centre

A "purist" underground techno den inspired by Berlin's nightlife — dark, immersive, and built for house and techno purists.

Harringtons Cocktail Lounge
4.3 · District Six 61B Harrington St

A boutique restaurant that transforms into a vibrant dance floor after midnight — free entry before 10 PM, upscale cocktail menu.

The Athletic Club & Social
4.5 · City Bowl 35 Buitengracht St

A versatile three-storey venue with a velvet-draped basement — each floor plays something different, from jazz to deep house.

Drama Bar
Long Street Long St, City Centre

A high-energy Long Street institution, consistently one of the street's most popular "bubbling" late-night spots.

Cubana
Green Point / Foreshore Green Point, Cape Town

A Latino-themed social café and bar known for a lively atmosphere, a diverse cocktail menu, and a proper see-and-be-seen vibe.

Kind Regards
City Bowl City Centre, Cape Town

A trendy, intimate city spot focused on curated music and a stylish local crowd.

18+ Adult Venue
Mavericks Revue Bar
City Centre City Centre, Cape Town

Cape Town's premier upmarket strip club and revue bar — three floors, five stages, VIP lounges and private dance booths. Adult entertainment venue, strictly 18+; not included in the standard route, available as an optional add-on.

Mavericks Revue Bar is an 18+ adult entertainment venue and is offered as an optional add-on only, at the group's request — it is not part of the standard three-venue guestlist route.

Rules & Requirements

A physical South African ID or original passport is strictly mandatory for entry at every venue. Digital copies are not accepted.
The legal minimum is 18, but several CBD lounges (including HALO) enforce a 21+ or 23+ door policy. Mavericks Revue Bar is strictly 18+ and photo ID will be checked at the door.
Smart-casual attire is generally required — sportswear and flip-flops can mean denied entry at premium venues like HALO or Harringtons.
Table or VIP booth bookings at venues like HALO are often required for seating and are quoted separately from this package if requested.

What to Bring

  • Original South African ID or physical passport
  • Smart-casual outfit — no sportswear or flip-flops
  • Cash or card for extra rounds and table bookings
Not Included
  • Table/VIP booth bookings
  • Extra drinks beyond the two included
  • Mavericks Revue Bar (optional add-on, 18+)

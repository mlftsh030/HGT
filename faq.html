Riders must be at least 16 years old to drive a quad bike on our tours. Children aged 4–12 can join as a ride-along passenger on a two-seater bike with a guide or parent, and children under 4 cannot enter the reserve. Riders are limited to a maximum weight of 140kg.

We offer three riding times at Atlantis Dunes: 30 minutes (R550pp), 45 minutes (R750pp), and 60 minutes (R900pp). All three include a safety briefing, practice run, helmet and gear, and an experienced guide — the conservation permit (R250pp) is billed separately.
No prior experience is required. Every tour starts with a full safety briefing and a practice run on flat ground, and our professional, highly experienced guides lead the whole ride.
